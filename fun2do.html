Task

Our objective was to develop a Content Management System (CMS) for the applications website and concurrently design a web native android mobile application for users .

Strategy

Our initial objective was to develop a Content Management System CMS) to serve as a robust backend for the mobile application which included establishing the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) for internal users by engaging our dedicated UX/UI team. o Additionally, our plan included providing software development services to develop the android mobile application based on the comprehensive user experience and interface (UX/UI) conceptualized, designed, developed and provided by the client

Solution

We have developed an exceptional design for the mobile applications content management system (CMS) and designed a dedicated android mobile applicaon, based on the clients requirements and design, that significantly enhances the user experience for their students, contributing to an overall improvement in usability and functionality

additional info

The solution offers an innovative after-school revision program for Indian school students, utilizing an advanced gamified mastery learning model. This approach seamlessly blends the captivting world of gaming with the effectiveness of top-tier educators and the opportunity-matching capabilities of renowned aggregators. By combining these elements, the application fosters high student engagement, recognizing that engagement is a critical factor in driving learning outcomes such as completion and proficiency. Its a recognition that even the most well-produced content can fall short in delivering results if students are not actively engaged in the learning process. The application bridges this gap by making learning fun, easy, and accessible anytime, empowering students to achieve mastery in Math and Science as a valuable complement to their schoolwork
